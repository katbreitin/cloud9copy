Mademoiselle Caroline Rivierè

by Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres

The portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Riviere was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres, and today hangs in the Lourve. It is the third of three portraits of the Riviere family the artist has painted that year

Date: 1806
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimension: 100 x 70 cm
Home: Musee du Lourve Paris
